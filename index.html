About Me

I'm a word wrangler and chaos whisperer, turning caffeine, scattered thoughts, and occasional bad puns into stories that try to be clever, sometimes succeed, and always entertain. I overthink, overuse metaphors, and occasionally make dangling participles dance but hey, that's part of the fun. Writing is my way of turning my inner chaos into something the world can read, laugh at, and maybe even relate to.
